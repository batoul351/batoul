# flutter_resturant_app

An interactive restaurant app designed to deliver a smooth and delightful experience for users. Built with modern technologies to meet customer needs with ease and comfort.

## Key Features

- **Login & Sign Up**: Simple and secure interfaces for user authentication.
- **Account Verification**: Via email to ensure account safety.
- **Browse Menu & Meals**: Explore a beautifully organized food menu.
- **User Profile**
- **Table Reservation**: Choose date, time of guests with flexibility.
- **Add to Favorites**: Save your favorite meals for quick access.
- **Meal Rating**: Share your feedback and rate the dishes you try.
- **Special Offers**: Discover the latest discounts and seasonal deals.
- **Contact the Restaurant**: Direct contact info (phone).
